ENN TEAM PHOTOS — ANCHORS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Drop headshot photos for the anchors section into this folder.

FILE NAMING:
  Use simple lowercase filenames with no spaces.
  Examples:
    jane-smith.jpg
    john-doe.png
    anchor1.jpg

PHOTO TIPS:
  • Square crop works best (1:1 ratio)
  • Minimum size: 300x300 pixels
  • JPG or PNG format
  • Keep file size under 2MB

AFTER UPLOADING:
  Open  EDIT/06-TEAM.js  and update the photo field
  for the matching person. Example:
    photo: 'img/team/anchors/jane-smith.jpg'
