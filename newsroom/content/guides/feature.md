# Feature

A short story with a beginning, middle, and end about a person or a trend at Eastlake. Target ~2–3 minutes.

## Do
- Find the human angle — one person the story is really about.
- Shoot plenty of B-roll of them doing the thing.

## Don't
- Narrate what the viewer can already see.
- Bury the point past the first 20 seconds.
