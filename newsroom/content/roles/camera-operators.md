# Camera Operators (×2)

> "The camera doesn't lie."

You capture the picture the whole show is built on. Clean framing and safe footage are non-negotiable.

## What you own
- Operate the studio cameras.
- Keep clean, consistent framing.
- **Verify SD cards are formatted with space before every shoot.**
- Monitor focus and exposure.
- Communicate silently with the Studio Director.
- Back up footage.
- Set up and break down.
- Grab B-roll whenever there's a gap.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Learn the silent hand signals and the SD-card routine first. A formatted, backed-up card is the difference between a show and a disaster.
