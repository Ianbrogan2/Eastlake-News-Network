# Newsroom Director

> "Every segment that airs reflects your editorial judgment."

You decide what's good enough to air, and you keep the week's content varied and on schedule.

## What you own
- Review, evaluate, and **approve all student segments**.
- Schedule segments across the week for variety, pacing, and quality.
- Send revisions and re-shoots when a piece isn't ready.
- Oversee equipment check-outs and the log.
- Resolve QC before content ever reaches the editor.
- Enforce content standards — if it can't air, it doesn't move forward.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Learn the pitch board and equipment log first. Sit in on a week of approvals with the outgoing director before you start approving solo.
