# Equipment Manager

> "Lost equipment costs everyone a broadcast."

Every camera, mic, and SD card is your responsibility. When gear walks off, shows don't happen.

## What you own
- Account for **all** gear.
- Log every check-out and check-in.
- Ensure on-time, working returns.
- Run inventory.
- Report damage immediately.
- Keep the equipment room labeled and clean.
- Track batteries, SD cards, mics, and kits.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Do a full inventory in week one and reconcile it against the Equipment table. If a count is off, find it before it becomes a missing-gear emergency.
