# Main Editor

> "What you export is what the school sees."

The final assembly runs through you. You turn a pile of clips into a ten-minute show.

## What you own
- Start editing **9:30 AM**.
- Assemble and edit the full daily bulletin.
- Export at the correct format and resolution.
- Upload to YouTube on time.
- Trim, time, and sequence segments.
- Add lower thirds, graphics, and transitions; monitor audio throughout.
- Keep project files and the folder structure organized.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Confirm the export preset and upload access on day one. Cut one full practice episode before you're on the live rotation.
