# Assistant Directors (×2)

> "Every group produces because you never stop moving."

You're the connective tissue between the desk and the groups. If a group is behind, you know first.

## What you own
- Circulate among groups every period.
- Collect completed segments and SD cards on time.
- Coach groups on framing, audio, and direction.
- Report progress to the Newsroom Director.
- Flag groups falling behind before it's a crisis.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Learn every group's members and air day in week one. Your value is knowing the status of the whole floor at any moment.
