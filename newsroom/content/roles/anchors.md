# Anchors (A1 & A2)

> "You are ENN to everyone watching."

You're the face of the network. Poise, preparation, and clean delivery are the job.

## What you own
- Write, proof, and finalize anchor copy **before 9:00 AM**.
- Film intro, transitions, and outro **by 9:30**.
- Deliver on camera with poise; adapt live when things change.
- Dress professionally.
- Rehearse on the teleprompter.

## Rotation
Anchors rotate **every two weeks**. Next week's anchors are announced each **Tuesday**.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Practice a full read on the teleprompter before your first show. Read the standards & dress code in the Newsroom section.
