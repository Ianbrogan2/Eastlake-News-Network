# Studio Director

> "The show runs on time because YOU run on time."

You are the command center of the daily broadcast. When the studio is chaos, you are the calm — and the clock.

## What you own
- Enforce the daily anchor filming schedule; **filming starts 9:00 AM sharp**.
- Guard audio fidelity — if it sounds bad, it doesn't air.
- Run teleprompter setup and timing.
- Direct anchors on pacing, presence, and delivery.
- Keep the whole team on schedule.
- Be the primary communications link between every role.

## Onboarding (new semester)
Read this page top to bottom, shadow the outgoing Studio Director for one week, and confirm you can run a full morning without help before your first solo show.
