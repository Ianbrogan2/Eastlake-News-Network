## Can it air?
If it embarrasses a student, isn't school-appropriate, or you couldn't show it to a parent — it doesn't air. When in doubt, ask the Newsroom Director before you shoot.

## Professional dress
Anchors and on-camera talent dress the part — clean, put-together, no logos that compete with ENN.

## Corrections policy
Got a fact wrong on air? Correct it on the next broadcast, plainly. Accuracy over ego.
