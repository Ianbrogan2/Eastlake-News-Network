# The ENN Rubric

100 points across three roles, plus automatic deductions. Grade yourself before you submit.

## Producer — 40 pts
- Deadline met
- Length compliance
- Appropriateness
- Planning / pre-production quality
- Use of script / plan
- Organization
- Member contribution
- Correct category

## Director — 40 pts
- Creativity
- Innovation
- Visual quality
- Framing
- Professionalism
- Presentation / effort
- Entertainment value

## Editor — 20 pts
- Audio quality
- Formatting / resolution
- Upload method & file naming
- Technical polish

## Automatic deductions
Late work · poor audio · missing lower thirds · spelling mistakes · no B-roll (sports/reporting) · incorrect export · unprofessionalism · can't air · low quality.
