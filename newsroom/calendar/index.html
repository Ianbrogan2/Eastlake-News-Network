Fall 2026 · 60 bulletins · 10:31–10:41 AM · 20 per period

How it's built. A bulletin airs only on days whose bell schedule includes the 10:31–10:41 slot (schedule types A, B, and N). Periods rotate P1 → P4 → P6 down the list, landing exactly 20 each.
No bulletins on: Labor Day (Sep 7), Fall Break (Sep 21–Oct 2), Veterans Day (Nov 11), Thanksgiving week (Nov 23–27), finals & minimum days (Dec 14–18), and “Full Day” Tuesdays (Sep 8, Oct 6, Dec 14). Fridays have no bulletin — except Nov 13, an “N” schedule day that includes the slot, counted here as Period 4.
