LicensingRead before you use audio

We do not re-host licensed music, SFX, or footage on this page — licensed content usually can't be publicly redistributed, and a public page is exactly where that gets us in trouble. Instead, get media from the licensed sources below and keep it in the shared class drive (behind the curtain), not on the open site.

  • YouTube Audio Library — free music & SFX cleared for use
  • Freesound — check each sound's license
  • Pixabay Music — royalty-free
